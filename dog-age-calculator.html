How Dog Age Is Calculated

The common "multiply by 7" rule is only a rough estimate. Dogs age much faster during their first two years of life and then continue aging at a slower rate. This calculator uses a simplified method that better reflects typical canine development.

Why Dog Years Matter

Understanding your dog's age in human years can help you make informed decisions about nutrition, exercise, veterinary care, and overall wellness throughout different life stages.
