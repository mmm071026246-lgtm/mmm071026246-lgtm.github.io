How Dog Age Is Calculated

Dogs do not age at the same rate throughout their lives. They mature rapidly during their first two years and then age more gradually. Larger breeds generally have shorter lifespans and age faster than smaller breeds.

Why Dog Years Matter

Understanding your dog's age in human years can help you make informed decisions about nutrition, exercise, veterinary care, and overall wellness throughout different life stages.
