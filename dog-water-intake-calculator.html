Dog Water Intake Calculator

Estimate your dog's daily water needs based on body weight. Water requirements can vary depending on temperature, activity level, diet, and overall health.



Daily Water Requirements

Healthy dogs typically need between 50 and 60 milliliters of water per kilogram of body weight each day. This calculator uses 55 ml per kilogram as a general guideline.

Why Hydration Matters

Adequate hydration supports digestion, circulation, temperature regulation, and overall health. Always ensure your dog has access to fresh drinking water.
