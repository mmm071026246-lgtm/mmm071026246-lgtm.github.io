Cat Food Calculator

Estimate your cat's recommended daily food amount based on body weight. Results are estimates and should be adjusted according to age, health, activity level, and veterinary recommendations.



Feeding Guidelines

Most adult cats require approximately 20 to 30 grams of dry food per kilogram of body weight per day. Actual requirements may vary depending on lifestyle and diet type.

Why Proper Feeding Matters

Maintaining a healthy weight helps reduce the risk of obesity, diabetes, joint problems, and other health conditions in cats.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much food should a cat eat per day?

Most adult cats require approximately 20 to 30 grams of dry food per kilogram of body weight each day, although individual needs vary.

Do kittens need more food than adult cats?

Yes. Kittens require more calories and nutrients to support rapid growth and development.

How often should I feed my cat?

Many cat owners feed adult cats two meals per day, although feeding schedules can vary.

Can overfeeding cause obesity?

Yes. Overfeeding is one of the most common causes of obesity in cats and may increase the risk of diabetes and joint problems.

Should I follow my veterinarian's advice?

Yes. This calculator provides estimates only and should not replace professional veterinary guidance.

Can I use this calculator for kittens?

Kittens have different nutritional requirements than adult cats. This calculator is intended primarily for adult cats and provides general estimates only.
