Estimate your cat's recommended daily food amount based on body weight. Results are estimates and should be adjusted according to age, health, activity level, and veterinary recommendations.



Feeding Guidelines

Most adult cats require approximately 20 to 30 grams of dry food per kilogram of body weight per day. Actual requirements may vary depending on lifestyle and diet type.

Why Proper Feeding Matters

Maintaining a healthy weight helps reduce the risk of obesity, diabetes, joint problems, and other health conditions in cats.
