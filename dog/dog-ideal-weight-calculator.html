Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my dog is at a healthy weight?

A healthy dog usually has a visible waist and ribs that can be felt without excessive pressure.

Can breed affect ideal weight?

Yes. Healthy weight ranges vary significantly between breeds.

Is this calculator accurate?

This calculator provides a general estimate only and should not replace veterinary advice.
