How Long Are Dogs Pregnant?

The average dog pregnancy lasts around 63 days from conception. Depending on breed, litter size, and individual factors, the delivery date may vary by several days.

Dog Pregnancy Timeline

  • Weeks 1-2: Fertilization and early embryo development.
  • Weeks 3-4: Pregnancy can often be confirmed by a veterinarian.
  • Weeks 5-6: Puppies develop rapidly and weight gain becomes noticeable.
  • Weeks 7-8: Nesting behavior may begin.
  • Week 9: Delivery is expected.

Pregnancy Care Tips

Provide high-quality nutrition, regular veterinary care, and a stress-free environment throughout pregnancy. Always contact your veterinarian if you notice unusual symptoms.

Important: This calculator provides an estimate only and should not replace professional veterinary advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long are dogs pregnant?

Most dogs are pregnant for approximately 63 days from conception.

Can the due date be inaccurate?

Yes. Actual delivery dates may vary by several days depending on the individual dog.

When should I see a veterinarian?

Regular veterinary checkups are recommended throughout pregnancy, especially if complications are suspected.

Does breed size affect pregnancy length?

Gestation length is generally similar across breeds, although minor variations can occur.
