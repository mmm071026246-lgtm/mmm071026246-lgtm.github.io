Frequently Asked Questions

How long do dogs usually live?

Most dogs live between 10 and 13 years, although lifespan varies by breed and size.

Do small dogs live longer than large dogs?

Yes. Small dog breeds generally live longer than large and giant breeds.

Is this calculator accurate?

This calculator provides estimates only and should not be considered a prediction of actual lifespan.
