How Long Are Dogs Pregnant?

The average dog pregnancy lasts around 63 days from conception. Depending on breed, litter size, and individual factors, the delivery date may vary by a few days.

Pregnancy Care Tips

Proper nutrition, regular veterinary checkups, and a safe environment are important throughout pregnancy. If you notice unusual symptoms or have concerns about your dog's health, consult a licensed veterinarian.
