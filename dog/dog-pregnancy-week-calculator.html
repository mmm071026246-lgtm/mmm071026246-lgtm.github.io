Dog Pregnancy Week Calculator

Use this calculator to estimate how many weeks pregnant your dog is based on the mating date. Most canine pregnancies last approximately 63 days.




Dog Pregnancy Timeline

  • Week 1–2: Fertilization and embryo development
  • Week 3–4: Embryo implantation and early growth
  • Week 5–6: Rapid puppy development
  • Week 7–8: Noticeable abdominal enlargement
  • Week 9: Delivery preparation and whelping

Why Track Pregnancy Weeks?

Knowing your dog's current pregnancy stage can help you prepare for veterinary visits, nutritional changes, and the arrival of puppies.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a dog pregnancy?

Most dog pregnancies last approximately 63 days from conception.

Can the due date vary?

Yes. Actual delivery dates may vary by several days depending on breed and individual factors.

When can a veterinarian confirm pregnancy?

Pregnancy can often be confirmed by ultrasound around 25 to 30 days after breeding.
