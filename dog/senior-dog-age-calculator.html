Frequently Asked Questions

When is a dog considered a senior?

Many dogs are considered seniors between 7 and 10 years of age depending on breed and size.

Do large dogs become seniors earlier?

Yes. Large and giant breeds often age faster and may become seniors earlier.

Why calculate senior dog age?

Understanding senior status can help owners provide appropriate care and nutrition.
